Aerodynamics is one of the most important aspects of vehicle design. This study shows how the rear shape of a vehicle affects aerodynamic interactions between the car body and the wheels. Using different car models in a wind tunnel, they were able to analyze pressure and airflow measurements. The results showed that while the effects on the wheels don't change much with shapes, the overall wake structure and drag are more sensitive with certain shapes.

Work Cited Bao, Di et al. “Influence of Vehicle Back Shape on Wheel-Vehicle Aerodynamic Interactions: A Model Study.” Experiments in fluids 65.4 (2024): n. pag. Web.
